Pizza Recipe

Pizza Crust Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt

Ingredients(Yeilds 8 servings)

  • 1 cup warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 ½ cups bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

The final steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C) and grease a pizza pan.
  2. Place warm water in a bowl. Add yeast and sugar, mix and let stand until creamy (about 10 minutes).
  3. Add flour, oil, and salt to the yeast mixture and mix until smooth.
  4. Let dough rest for 5 minutes.
  5. Roll out dough into a 12-inch circle on a lightly floured surface.
  6. Place on pan, add sauce and toppings.
  7. Bake 15–20 minutes until golden brown. Let cool before serving.
